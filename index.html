Authenticity & Sourcing

Japan's Strictest Standard

1
AACD-Certified Auction Sourcing

All products are purchased exclusively from auctions certified by AACD — Japan's leading organization dedicated to eliminating counterfeit goods from the secondary market.

2
In-House Expert Inspection

Two in-house specialists, each with 10+ years of experience, inspect every item for authenticity and grade it for condition before listing.

3
Optional Third-Party Certificate

For additional assurance, we can arrange an Entrupy-backed Certificate of Authenticity with a 1-year financial guarantee upon request.

4
Government-Licensed Operation

Jolijou Wholesale (TAKUMI Inc.) is fully licensed under Japan's Secondhand Goods Dealers Act. License No. 62123R042823.
